Laila Sahyoun

Laila Sahyoun, founder of Strategic Legal, is a business lawyer with over 25 years of experience in guiding Canadian entrepreneurs through mergers and acquisitions, financing rounds, and commercial negotiations. She started her legal career in the business law group of a national Canadian law firm followed by her role as senior legal counsel in multinational corporations. Her strong educational background and diverse legal experience provides her with the expertise to work on a variety of business law mandates, which she provides with enthusiasm and versatility.

Having grown up in an entrepreneurial family, she is known for her strategic thinking, rigour and strong negotiation skills. She built Strategic Legal to offer agile and client-centered legal support for growth-stage companies.
